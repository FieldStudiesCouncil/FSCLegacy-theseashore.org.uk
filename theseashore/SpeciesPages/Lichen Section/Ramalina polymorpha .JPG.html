Ramalina polymorpha

Ramalina polymorpha – Thallus fruticose, (tufted , erect) greenish grey, more or less flattened branches with similar upper and lower surface . Similar to Ramalina fruticosa but broader branches, slightly inrolled and covered in rows of warts. Rarely fertile. Rare lusitanean species found almost exclusively on isolated, bird perching rocks with high nitrate requirement.
